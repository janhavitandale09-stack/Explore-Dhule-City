Emergency Services in Dhule

Important Emergency Services

Hospitals

District Civil Hospital and Shri Bhausaheb Hire Government Medical College provide major medical services in Dhule. Many private hospitals and clinics are also available for emergency treatment.

Police Services

Dhule City Police Station, Deopur Police Station and Sakri Road Police Station maintain law and order in the city. For any emergency or crime related issue people can call the police helpline number 100.

Fire Brigade

The Dhule Municipal Fire Station provides fire safety and rescue services in case of fire accidents. People can contact the fire department quickly by dialing the emergency number 101.

Ambulance

Ambulance services are available in Dhule for medical emergencies and patient transport. The national ambulance helpline number 108 can be dialed for immediate medical assistance.

Women Helpline

Women safety services are available through special helpline numbers. Women facing any emergency or safety issue can contact the helpline number 181.

Disaster Management

Disaster management authorities help during natural disasters and emergency situations. People in Dhule can call the disaster helpline number 1077 for assistance.
