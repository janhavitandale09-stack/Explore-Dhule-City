Culture of Dhule

Festivals & Traditional Culture of Dhule

Sambal Dance

Sambal dance is a traditional folk dance of the Khandesh region. It is performed with the powerful beats of the Sambal drum during village festivals and temple fairs.

Holi Festival

Holi is celebrated with bright colors, music, and joyful gatherings in Dhule. People come together to enjoy traditional sweets and celebrate the arrival of spring.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi is one of the biggest festivals celebrated in Dhule. Beautiful idols of Lord Ganesha are installed and cultural programs and processions are organized.

Village Jatra

Village Jatra is a traditional temple fair held in many villages around Dhule. These fairs include religious rituals, local food stalls, and folk performances.

Balaji Rathotsav

Balaji Rathotsav is an important religious festival celebrated in Dhule. During this festival, a decorated chariot carrying the idol of Lord Balaji is taken in a grand procession.

Ahirani Folk Culture

Ahirani is the traditional language spoken in the Khandesh region. Folk songs, dances, and customs of Ahirani culture are an important part of Dhule's identity.
