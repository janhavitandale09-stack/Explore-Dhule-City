Explore Dhule – Attractions
Top Attractions of Dhule District

Laling Fort

Laling Fort is one of the most important historical landmarks near Dhule. Built during the Faruqi dynasty, this hilltop fort offers panoramic views of the surrounding region.

Aner Dam

Aner Dam is a peaceful reservoir surrounded by natural beauty. During monsoon the overflowing water creates a scenic and refreshing environment.

Swaminarayan Mandir

Swaminarayan Mandir in Dhule is a beautiful and peaceful temple known for its detailed architecture and spiritual atmosphere.

Vastu Sangrahalay

Vastu Sangrahalay in Dhule is a local museum that preserves historical artifacts, sculptures and traditional objects from the region.

Panzreshwar Mahadev

Panzreshwar Mahadev Temple is a sacred Shiva temple surrounded by natural beauty and visited by devotees during festivals.

Icchapurti Ganpati

Icchapurti Ganpati Temple is a famous religious place where devotees believe their wishes are fulfilled.
