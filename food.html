Famous Food of Dhule

Popular Dishes

Shev Bhaji

Shev Bhaji is one of the most famous dishes of the Khandesh region, including Dhule. It is a spicy curry made from gram flour noodles (shev) cooked in a flavorful onion, tomato, and garlic gravy with traditional Khandeshi spices.

Khandeshi Chicken

Khandeshi Chicken is one of the most famous and spicy dishes from the Khandesh region, including Dhule. It is cooked with special Khandeshi masala, dry red chillies, garlic, and traditional spices, which give it a strong and rich flavor.

Varan Batti

Varan Batti is a traditional Khandeshi meal made of wheat flour balls (batti) that are baked or roasted and served with varan (a simple dal made from toor dal). It is usually eaten with ghee, chutney, and onion, giving it a rich and authentic taste.

Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a famous traditional sweet dish in Maharashtra and is also very popular in Dhule. It is a soft flatbread stuffed with a sweet mixture of jaggery and chana dal (puran) and flavored with cardamom.

Khichdi

Khichdi is a simple and healthy dish commonly eaten in Dhule and across Maharashtra. It is made by cooking rice and lentils together with mild spices, making it soft and easy to digest. It is usually served with ghee, pickle, papad, or curd, and is often eaten as a comfort meal at home.

Vangyach Bharit

Vangyach Bharit is a traditional Maharashtrian dish that is very popular in the Khandesh region, including Dhule. It is made from roasted brinjal (eggplant) which is mashed and mixed with onion, garlic, green chilli, and spices.
