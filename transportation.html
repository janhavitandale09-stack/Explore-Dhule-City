Transportation in Dhule

Transportation Facilities in Dhule

Air Transportation

Dhule Gondur Airport is located near Gondur village, about 8 km from Dhule city in Maharashtra.

It is mainly used for pilot training and small aircraft operations, and currently has no regular commercial flights.

Road Transportation

Dhule is connected by major highways like NH-3 (Mumbai–Agra Highway) and NH-6.

MSRTC buses and private buses connect Dhule to cities like Mumbai, Pune, Nashik and Surat.

Railway Transportation

Dhule Railway Station connects the district with nearby junctions such as Chalisgaon and Manmad.

Rail transport is used for passengers and also for transporting agricultural products.

Local Transportation

Auto-rickshaws are the most common local transport in Dhule city.

People also use bikes, taxis and private vehicles for daily travel and tourism.
